Community Rules

General Guidelines

  1. Understand Before You Upload: Please review existing posts and familiarize yourself with our formatting standards before submitting your schematic.
  2. Post Original Work: Upload only your own creations. If you’re sharing someone else’s work, you must include proper and visible credit.
  3. List Dependencies: If your schematic requires specific mods, plugins, or assets, clearly list all dependencies in your post.
  4. Use English Only: All descriptions, titles, and comments must be written in English to ensure accessibility for everyone.
  5. Maintain Quality: Low-effort or incomplete uploads may be removed at the discretion of site moderators.
  6. Be Respectful: Keep discussions in the comments civil and constructive.
  7. No Impersonation: Do not create accounts pretending to be other users, creators, or staff members.
  8. Test Before Uploading: Verify that your schematic works as intended before posting it.
  9. Accurate Thumbnails: Thumbnails must clearly represent the schematic being uploaded.
  10. Respect Ownership: Do not redistribute content uploaded by others without explicit permission.

Bannable Offenses

All bans are final and linked to IP addresses. Illegal activity will be reported to law enforcement.

  1. Manipulating Ratings: Do not attempt to artificially inflate or deflate schematic ratings.
  2. Posting NSFW Content: Explicit, sexual, or otherwise inappropriate material is strictly prohibited.
  3. Advertising: Do not promote external sites, services, or communities (for example, Discord servers).
  4. Malicious Uploads: Uploading files containing viruses, malware, or other harmful code will result in an immediate and permanent ban.
